Making an offer of employment letter for my small business ‘Buildify’.
This letter was one of my very first times making a formal business letter of any kind or sort. From this artifact, I developed skills of letter writing especially in business. I vividly remember learning about the several and specific rules of letter writing, like the amount of spaces you should have, and where you should always locate or write in things such as the date or the sender’s name, in this case, me.

This letter was meant to be formally and professionally written, which is a good practice of learning how to keep my private and professional work life separate. I also learned about the various areas of recruitment and HR management in a business context.
