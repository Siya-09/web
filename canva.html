Then, we also researched plagiarism in the workplace making me come to a realization that it is an extremely serious offense and many people including a news reporter from CNN have gotten fired from doing so even a little bit.

I have learned that I shouldnt plagiarize, why not, and even applied these teachings to the presentation. I made sure to cite and provide links to all of the resources I used to research and wrote everything in my own words. Moreover, I also learned to use my time wisely while doing my research and working on my good copy in class, communication, and more about making presentations efficiently and as quickly as possible.
