This article was a project we had to do that focused on ergonomics, and comfortability in the workplace.
We had to think about our chosen business; mine being Buildify, a small-scale furniture company that started in our very own basement. When it came to my business, I thought that my chosen furniture would be a great addition to my new workplace. This included a standing desk, a chair made for office workers, a mouse that eases the pressure on your wrists, an ergonomic split keyboard, a high-resolution monitor, and a set of productive headphones.

The four of us each had a part in making the presentation, and researched for our work. Completing this presentation and doing my part allowed me to have an increase of an understanding in more complex concepts, improved communication skills, collaboration skills, problem-solving, and even technical skills regarding Fluix’s technical capabilities.
