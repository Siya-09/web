This was a group presentation we did on a technology tool called Fluix.
Fluix is a software that helps companies automate their routine document processes in a more efficient manner than usual. Fluix allows one to streamline document workflows, reduce manual errors, improve productivity, collaborate with your team, capture, analyze or store data in an organized manner, have a secure base, and keep your organization’s image as a professional company
