Business group letter and infographic assignment
For this assignment, we had to organize a staff picnic for our employees to have a relaxing environment, and encourage them to work well later on. It was a group project, where we made 2 letters, one to the employees and one to our CEO to invite them to our spring picnic, as well as an interactive poster to advertise it.

The letters inviting our CEO and employees to the springtime staff picnic were especially a good example of my skills in several areas such as collaboration, event planning, being able to switch from formal to more casual letters, inclusivity, networking building, and even motivational strategies. Moreover, I believe a significant part of being an office worker is being able to switch from formal communication to casual communication, or anything in between with ease. This is because I will be talking to several types of people with different jobs who have different relationships with me.
