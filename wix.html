What is AIDA?

Attention, Interest, Desire, and Action, or AIDA, is a fundamental marketing concept that is used to create messages or campaigns that are compelling. It describes a step-by-step method for interacting with an audience: drawing them in, piquing their curiosity about the good or service, creating a desire for it, and then encouraging them to take an action, such as buying something or signing up for a service. Marketers can create persuasive messages that successfully assist customers in making decisions by adhering to the AIDA model.
